This is the second in the series, following on from the previous work, which was well received.
This time around, the album includes a number of remixes from overseas, making it even more powerful.
At the same time, a number of songs from works related to the Sonic Team have been picked up.

1. WORK IT OUT ...Eric Kupper 7' Classic Mix - from SONIC R
 2. LOVE & PEACE ...Groove That Soul Mix - from SPIKE OUT
3. WE ARE BURNING RANGERS ...Body & Soul Vocal - from BURNING RANGERS
 4. THEME OF ENDING ...KANAMIX - from WATER SKI
5. WORK IT OUT ...Hex Hector Dub - from SONIC R
 6. ATC - domination ...HITOMIX - from LEMANS 24
7. DREAMS DREAMS ...Roland Clark Main Mix - from NiGHTS
 8. SONA MI ARERU EC SANCITU ...Prescription Vocal Club Mix - from AZEL [Panzer Dragoon RPG]
9. I JUST SMILE ...Maurice's Happy Smile Mix - from BURNING RANGERS
